# Cloud Operations

The cloud is only as good as the way you operate it. EADX brings deep expertise in cloud operations to help enterprises run reliable, efficient, and secure cloud environments.

## What We Do

We manage and optimize your cloud infrastructure so you can focus on your core business. Our cloud operations services cover everything from migration and setup to day-to-day management and continuous improvement.

## Our Approach

- **Cloud Assessment & Migration** — We evaluate your workloads and plan a smooth migration path to the cloud
- **Managed Operations** — Our team monitors, manages, and optimizes your cloud environment around the clock
- **Automation & DevOps** — We implement infrastructure-as-code, CI/CD pipelines, and automation to increase efficiency and reduce risk

## Technologies

We operate across major cloud platforms including Microsoft Azure, AWS, and Google Cloud, with expertise in containerization, Kubernetes, Terraform, and modern DevOps toolchains.
