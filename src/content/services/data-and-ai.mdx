# Data & AI

Data is the foundation of intelligent business. EADX helps enterprises unlock the value of their data assets through modern data platforms, analytics, and AI-driven solutions.

## What We Do

We build and operate data platforms that turn raw information into strategic advantage. From data engineering and warehousing to advanced analytics and AI integration, we help you make faster, better-informed decisions.

## Our Approach

- **Data Strategy & Governance** — We define your data architecture and governance framework to ensure quality, security, and compliance
- **Data Engineering** — Our team builds scalable data pipelines and platforms that consolidate information from across your enterprise
- **Analytics & AI** — We implement analytics solutions and AI capabilities that deliver measurable business outcomes

## Technologies

We leverage leading platforms including Databricks, Snowflake, Microsoft Azure Data Services, and various AI/ML frameworks to build solutions tailored to your needs.
